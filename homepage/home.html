About us

Tullu Dimtu Primary school is known for providing best education for students from 1st to 8th grade. We use student based learning system to make our students creative and help them to develop their skills. Long time experience of teaching make our teachers special. Our mission is to develop intellectual,ethical,creativity foundation on our students to create community for better world.
